Banking Sector

Banking Institution Licensing

Regulated by Bank of Botswana under the Banking Act

Commercial Bank License

Bank of Botswana 90-120 day process
  • Minimum capital: BWP 100 million
  • Full banking services
  • Deposit-taking authority

Non-Bank Sector

Non-Bank Financial Institution Licensing

Regulated by NBFIRA under the NBFIRA Act

Insurance License

NBFIRA 60-90 day process
  • Minimum capital: BWP 10 million
  • Risk-based capital requirements
  • Actuarial certification required
View Details

Microfinance License

NBFIRA 45-60 day process
  • Minimum capital: BWP 5 million
  • Limited deposit-taking
  • Community lending focus
View Details

Collective Investment License

NBFIRA 75-100 day process
  • Minimum capital: BWP 2.5 million
  • CIS manager requirements
  • Trustee arrangement needed
View Details

Pension Fund License

NBFIRA 90-120 day process
  • Minimum capital: BWP 7.5 million
  • Actuarial valuation required
  • Strict governance standards
View Details
